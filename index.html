Overview

The 1.20 Trails & Tales update introduced the Cherry Grove biome to Minecraft, which happens to be the entire reason I created this realm. I wanted a fresh start with pink wood, yay!!!! The one color the community had begged for since 2020 (the Nether wood update)! Because of this, we made an entire pink and white mansion (also thanks to the Pale Forest update), and were inspired to create an entire pink, cottage-core, bright and saturated world. Filled with flowers fields, colored concrete, cherry blossom trees, whimsical and fun builds, accompanied by very long trips to the Nether for glowstone, froglights, and shroomlights.
